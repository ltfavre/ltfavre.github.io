About Me

I’m LaToya Favre, PhD, a clinical psychologist turned behavioral data scientist and clinical informatics professional. My background includes years of direct clinical work, psychological assessment, and report-writing, now combined with advanced analytics and automation.

I specialize in translating messy, real-world clinical data into clear insights and tools that clinicians can actually use — from streamlined assessment workflows to predictive models that flag risk and support better decision-making.

I’m particularly interested in mental-health outcomes, usability of digital tools, and equitable access to care. My work aims to support both patients and providers by improving the systems that connect them.
